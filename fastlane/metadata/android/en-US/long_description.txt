# Floppy Warriors

A janky ragdoll archery game (scalables later). Battle against AI opponents in physics-based arrow combat!

## How to Play

### Gameplay
1. Aim with your mouse cursor
2. Hold left click or touch to charge your shot - the bow glows redder as power increases
3. Release to fire arrows at the enemy
4. Hit the enemy to deal damage and apply knockback
5. Defeat enemies to earn 10 points each (might scale in the future)
6. Enemies get stronger (more health) as your score increases
7. If you fall off screen or lose all your health, you'll get sent to heaven!

### Misc. Tips
- Longer charge = more arrow power and speed
- Arrows stick to bodies on impact
- Use air dodge to avoid incoming arrows
- Watch both health bars in the corners of the screen

### Controls (With a mouse and keyboard)
- **Left Mouse Button (Hold)**: Draw bow - hold longer for more power
- **Left Mouse Button (Release)**: Fire arrow
- **Right Mouse Button / Spacebar**: Air dodge (push your ragdoll upward)
- **Mouse Movement**: Aim your bow
- **R**: Restart game after game over
- **S**: Open settings
- **F11**: Toggle fullscreen
